Work Rules! by Laszlo Bock

2020-07-11

Insights from Inside Google That Will Transform How You Live and Lead by Laszlo Bock

Work Rules! is an informative look at Google’s people management. After reading it, I am still not entirely convinced that Google’s approach is applicable to every company but the lessons below certainly are.

Culture > strategy

Give people slightly more trust, freedom, and authority than you are comfortable with. If leaders aren’t nervous, they haven’t provided enough space for employees to surprise.

Hiring

Every company has limited resources. Take the time to hire only the best. Hire only those better than you in a meaningful way. Do not let managers make hiring decision for their teams — assess candidates objectively.

Finding the best talent

Get the best referrals and be very specific in defining requirements. Make recruiting everyone’s job.

Empowering employees

Eliminate status symbols. Make data-driven decision. Have high expectations.

Performance management

Take the time to set goals correctly. Gather peer feedback and use a calibration process to finalize rating. Split rewards and development conversations.

Build a learning institution

Have the best people teach and place them under a microscope. Apply how your best people operate broadly. Invest only in education that provably changes people’s behavior.

Pay unfairly

Wide variations in pay are necessary to reflect the power law of performance. Emphasize accomplishment, not compensation. Reward failure when well thought out.
